How It Works

Get started in 3 simple steps. No complex setup required.

1

Generate QR Code

Admin logs into the dashboard and generates a unique QR code for their business with customizable branding.

2

Customer Scans

Customer scans the QR code at your store using their mobile app and instantly earns points for their purchase.

3

Redeem Rewards

When customers reach their goal (e.g., 10 points), they automatically receive a free menu item or reward.

Unified API

Both web dashboard and mobile app use the same powerful API, ensuring consistency and real-time synchronization across all platforms.
